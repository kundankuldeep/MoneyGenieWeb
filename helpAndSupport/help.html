Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Money Genie?

Money Genie is a financial management app that helps users track expenses, set budgets, and manage their savings efficiently.

2. How do I create an account?

You can create an account using your email or social media login. Simply follow the sign-up process in the app.

3. Is my financial data secure?

Yes, Money Genie uses advanced encryption and security measures to keep your financial data safe.

4. Can I link my bank account to the app?

Yes, you can securely link your bank account to track transactions and monitor your expenses in real time.

5. What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions to reset your password.

6. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach out to us through the Contact Us page for further assistance.
